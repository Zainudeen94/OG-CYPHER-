My Story - Zainudeen

I am Zainudeen, a certified Computer Engier, Micrisoft Server Admininistrator, Network specialist and an aspiring developer. I'm the founder of Deentech IT Solutions.

I started my journey like many others--full of ambition, facing real-life struggles, and trying to find my place in a noisy world. I grew up in Ghana and attended Ngleshie Amanfro Senior High before pursuing IT certifications from City and Guilds of London. I'm currently furthering my studies at AIT Univeristy, and even on days when I have just three hours of class, I don't sit idle-- I go out and work.

When I'm not in the classroom or working on tech solutions, I split my time between the Kasoa Timber Market and Ampotech, where I put my hands to work to earn a living and support my vision. Whether it's lifting timber or suppporting operational tasks at Ampotech, I believe in staying productive, grounded, and relentless in pursuit of my goals.

Yes, even as a certified IT professional, I do what it takes to make end meet-because survival comes before pride, and discipline fuels dreams.

From working at Lucky TV Ghana managing WordPress sites and social media, I have always believed that knowledge means nothing without action. My days may start with networks and end with lifting wood, but it's all part of the journey.

Today, I help clients solve IT problems, build better systems, and move forward with confidence. Behind every solution I provide is a story of resilence, realness, and relentless pursuit of excellence.
